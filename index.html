💡 Essential Tips
🚇

KLIA Ekspres — fastest way into KL

28 min from KLIA to KL Sentral, MYR 55pp. Book online for convenience. Way faster than Grab from the airport.

📱

Grab is your best friend

Use Grab for all taxis in Malaysia — safer, metered, and much cheaper than street taxis. Download before you land!

🏨

Same KL hotel for both stays

Book the same KLCC hotel for 17–18 Jun AND 22–24 Jun. Ask them to store your Langkawi bags for free on 19 Jun — lifesaver!

⛽

Langkawi is duty-free!

Alcohol, chocolates, perfumes are significantly cheaper. Stock up before flying back to KL — airport limits apply on the return.

🎢

Book Genting SkyWorlds online

Tickets are cheaper online than at the gate. Also skips the queue. And seriously — bring a jacket. Genting is COLD!

💧

Only drink bottled water

Tap water in Malaysia is technically treated but stick to bottled throughout. Buy big bottles from convenience stores — way cheaper than hotel mini-bars.

🌶️

Ask for "kurang pedas"

Means "less spicy" in Malay — Malaysian spice levels can catch Indians off guard! Say this at hawker stalls to get a milder version.

💸

Best money exchange in KL

Avoid airport money changers — terrible rates. Best rates at licensed money changers in Bukit Bintang area. Alternatively, withdraw MYR from local ATMs.

🛥️

Book sunset cruise in advance

Langkawi sunset cruises sell out in June (peak season). Book the day before at your hotel or via Klook / Viator. ~MYR 80pp — absolutely worth it!

🏛️

Temple dress code

Cover shoulders and knees at Batu Caves, Kek Lok Si, and Putra Mosque. Carry a sarong — many temples provide wraps but having your own is easier.
